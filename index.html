Experiment id: #23
PRIVACY & CONSENT CONTACT

In this experiment you will see circles appearing and disappearing at random locations inside the container on the left side

Try to click on them before they disappear.

The duration of display will be shorter periodically, until the first part of the experiment ends.

In the second part of the experiment you will see briefly + and - symbols on the center of the container.


Try to leftclick on the circle that comes after + sign...

...and try to rightclick on the circle that comes after - sign.

Wrong clicks won't change the score.

In the third and last part of the experiment the + and - symbols will be displayed inside arrow, that randomly point to left or right.

Ignore the direction the arrows point at and continue just like in the previous part.

At the end of the experiment you will be able to see your performance.
